Welcome to STUDENTPORTAL!

What does STUDENTPORTAL do?

STUDENTPORTAL is a student administration portal that lets you log into your school website to be able to access important information, as well as contain and provide you information on courses offered, transcripts, email programs, timetables, exam schedules, and department contact numbers.

Want to create a profile?

STUDENTPORTAL allows students to create profiles for themselves, along with a brief description that they can use to describe themselves!

Need to check your performance?

STUDENTPORTAL keeps track of your assignments for each class, as well as telling you your performance for each assignment!
